A completely separate, brand-new two-bedroom unit with its own entrance at our home in Hanley's Farm. Everything you need for a comfortable stay — your own bathroom, laundry, and full kitchen — with The Remarkables right outside the window.

## Your own space

The unit is completely self-contained with a dedicated entrance, so you come and go as you please. Inside you'll find two bedrooms, a bathroom, a kitchen with everything you need to cook, and your own laundry facilities.

## Location

Hanley's Farm sits on the edge of Jack's Point, with the golf course an easy walk away and The Remarkables ski field a short drive up the road. It's a quieter, mountain-side alternative to staying in Queenstown's CBD, without giving up easy access to everything the region offers.

We're close to the Remarkables Park shopping centre for groceries and essentials, and Queenstown's town centre is about 15 minutes by car.

## What's included

Everything you need for a self-catered stay: full kitchen, laundry, bathroom, WiFi, parking, and ski storage. We provide fresh linen, towels, and basic kitchen supplies to get you started.
